Finding hope amongst pain.

Cancer brought so many things to the surface: vulnerability, desperation, and an ache for answers that sometimes never come.

It also brought a new way of seeing God's faithfulness. I've learned through every doctor's appointment and long night that God meets us where we are.

We're never had to walk this alone.

Our church family, friends near and far, and even strangers have walked with us praying, dropping off meals, writing encouraging notes, and showing up in beautiful ordinary ways.

Hope, for me, is a choice every single day.

It isn't pretending everything is okay, but believing that God is bigger than every setback and every tear.

Our story isn't finished, and maybe yours isn't either.

Still, I know without a doubt that cancer may have changed the shape of our lives, but it could never diminish the love of Christ.
